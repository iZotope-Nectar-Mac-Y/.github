## 🖌 Overview  
iZotope Nectar Advanced is a complete vocal production suite that covers the entire vocal processing chain from pitch correction and timing through EQ, compression, de-essing, saturation, and reverb in a single integrated plugin interface.

AI-powered pitch correction analyzes and corrects vocal pitch with natural-sounding results that preserve the character of the performance, with adjustable correction speed and tolerance that allows everything from subtle tuning to hard correction.

Harmony generation creates up to four vocal harmony parts from a single vocal track, with pitch intervals, panning, and timing settings that produce natural-sounding harmonies without recording additional takes.

Vocal Assistant analyzes incoming vocal audio and automatically sets appropriate starting points for each processing module, providing an intelligent first pass that you refine rather than building a vocal chain from scratch every session.

For producers and mixing engineers who want professional vocal results quickly and want all the tools they need for vocal processing in one place, iZotope Nectar Advanced is the vocal suite that addresses every stage of the processing workflow.
